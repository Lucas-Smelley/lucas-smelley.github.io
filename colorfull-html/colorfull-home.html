ColorFull
Explore a scene of color, collect treasure,
avoid enemies, and change everything

What is it?

ColorFull is a game centered around customization. You are able to change most aspects of the game while playing. Experimenting with different settings and trying different combinations of settings to see whats cool is the main purpose of the game. There is also the optional challege of collecting treasures, and avoiding enemies, but these can be disabled.


Tutorial

Player

As soon as you begin, you will see a small white square in the center of the screen, this is the you. You are able to freely move around the screen using the WASD keys.
Treasure

When you begin, a treasure is spawned somewhere randomly on the map. The shadow on the outside of the game will become more red as you get closer to the treasure, and blue as you get farther from the treasure. Once you are close enough to the treasure, it will appear.
Enemies

As you player, triangular shaped enemies will begin to target you, and will follow you until they collide with you, ending the game. As time goes on, enemies will spawn more quickly over time, and their speed will increase as well. They can be toggled on and of by pressing 'e', or in settings.
Settings

The settings are a large part of ColorFull. Through the settings you can control almost every aspect of the game. You can open and close the settings by clicking on 'Settings' on the right side of the screen. Some of them are labeled, but others aren't.
